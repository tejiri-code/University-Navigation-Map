Education And Humanities Building(EAH)
Home Page

This building is where classes for students in the EAH department take place. This is where the offices of the GEDs lecturers are and also the location of the radio station.

Directions:

Once you enter the school , you go straight down and turn to your left at the first roundabout. It is located on the left hand side immediately after the roundabout.There is a huge signboard above it stating "School Of Education And Humanities".
