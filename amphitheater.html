Amphitheater

The amphitheater is one of the school's main auditorium.It is used to host quite a number of various events e.g Matriculation,Convocation, etc.

Directions:

Once you enter the school,you go straight down until the first roundabout then take the left turning and move straight down until you get to the first intersection. At that intersection, take a right turning to the end.There is a signboard that states "Amphitheater" in front of it.
