Science And Technology building(SAT)
Home Page

This is an academic building for the school of science and technology where both science labs are situated.Classes are held there.

Directions:

Once you enter the school,you go straight down until the first roundabout then take the left turning and move straight down until you get to the Babcock tower behind amphitheater .From the tower, walk down and turn right at the first intersection and left at the second intersection
